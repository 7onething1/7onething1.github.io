## The four reads

- **[Doom Prevails](https://drwu-htmls.vercel.app/salubrioussnail-doom-deaddraws)** — Reducing dead draws through synergy. Two engines (Doctor Doom's land-discard drain, Loki's copy loop); a card that feeds neither is a dead draw. Swaps: Superior Foes of Spider-Man → Green Goblin, Revenant · Molecule Man → Kingpin, Wilson Fisk · Damocles Base, Sword of Kang → Sundial of the Infinite.
- **[The Fantastic Four](https://drwu-htmls.vercel.app/salubrioussnail-ff-manabase)** — Mana fixing and color consistency. The only four-color deck; its one tax is color. Swaps: Willie Lumpkin, Postman → Talisman of Creativity · Mirage Mirror → Talisman of Conviction · Into the Time Vortex → West Coast Expansion.
- **[Avengers Assemble](https://drwu-htmls.vercel.app/salubrioussnail-avengers-simpler)** — The case for simpler decks. The friendliest deck wins by being easy to read. Swaps: Love on the Battlefield → Champions from Beyond · Avenge → Damning Verdict · Gift of Immortality → Wave Goodbye.
- **[Wakanda Forever](https://drwu-htmls.vercel.app/salubrioussnail-wakanda-wrongchoice)** — When a good card is the wrong choice. Raw power is one test among several. Swaps: Coveted Jewel → Batterskull · Gilded Lotus → Reprieve · Sword of the Animist → Verdurous Gearhulk.

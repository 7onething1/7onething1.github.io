Run-of-show checklist

The organizer's short list for a smooth night.

  • Post the poster and social captions one week out, then again the morning of.
  • Print table tents on card stock — one per table, plus a spare House Rules card at the counter.
  • Set out loaner precons and a printed page of the deck-tech hub links (or a QR to this page).
  • Seat pods of four by rough power level; keep at least one table flagged "new players welcome."
  • Open with a two-minute welcome: format, life total, and the "explain your combo" courtesy rule.
  • After the night, thank players in a follow-up post and tease the next commander on deck.

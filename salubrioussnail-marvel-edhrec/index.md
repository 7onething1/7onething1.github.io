- **EDHREC most-played:** Blasphemous Act · Toxic Deluge · Black Market Connections · Chaos Warp · Terminate · Monument to Endurance
- **Signature:** Doom's Time Platform (57%)

The high-synergy column reads like my dead-draw audit already ran: those are all Villains worth copying, the exact copy engine I lean on. The most-played list is clean interaction, so the crowd covers the board without adding noise. Monument to Endurance turning up here is a nice irony, the card I cut from a cycling deck doing honest card-advantage work in Doom. → [my full Doom read](https://drwu-htmls.vercel.app/salubrioussnail-doom-deaddraws)

## Every EDHREC Marvel article, my take

I am not reprinting anyone's writing; each line is my own read of the topic, grounded in the numbers above.

- **[Building Villain Typal with Loki, the Deceiver](https://edhrec.com/articles/building-villain-typal-with-loki-the-deceiver).** Loki is in Doom's high-synergy column for a reason. Run bodies worth copying twice over, and Doom's discard drain has more to work with.
- **[Top 10 Suspend Enablers for Kang Prime](https://edhrec.com/articles/top-10-suspend-enablers-for-kang-prime).** Kang is a box alt and a different engine from the discard drain. Pick one and build it fully; a half suspend package inside a discard deck leaves you drawing dead.
- **[The Over/Under: Predicting Marvel Commander Popularity, Part 2](https://edhrec.com/articles/the-over-under-predicting-the-popularity-of-marvel-super-heroes-commanders-part-2) and [Part 3](https://edhrec.com/articles/the-over-under-predicting-the-popularity-of-marvel-super-heroes-commanders-part-3).** The deck counts settle the bets: Captain America near 2,399, T'Challa around 2,068, Mister Fantastic close to 1,064. Popular is a different signal from powerful, worth remembering before you read a big count as a strong deck.

## The through-line

Across all four precons, EDHREC's data lines up with the reads I already wrote: Villains and clean removal for Doom, fetch-ramp fixing for the Fantastic Four, the Nick Fury counters engine for the Avengers, artifact voltron for T'Challa. Where the crowd differs, it usually solves the same problem with different hardware. The data gives you a second opinion, and here it mostly backs the plan.

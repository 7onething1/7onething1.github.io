The crowd builds T'Challa as artifact voltron, and the numbers make the identity plain. Vibranium Strike Gauntlets in eighty-three percent of decks, Hammer of Nazahn at eighty-two, and Helm of the Host at seventy-seven are all big artifacts that suit up a threat and, at four mana or more, feed T'Challa's counter trigger. That is the Vibranium-and-counters plan I said to protect, and it is why I put Verdurous Gearhulk into this deck: it is a five-mana artifact that triggers the commander and brings its own four counters. The through-lineThe crowd mostly agrees with the swaps

Read the numbers first, then decide. When my upgrade lens and the community's most-played lists point the same way, that is a good sign the plan is real.

Across all four precons, EDHREC's data lines up with the reads I already wrote: Villains and clean removal for Doom, fetch-ramp fixing for the Fantastic Four, the Nick Fury counters engine for the Avengers, and artifact voltron for T'Challa. Where the crowd differs, it is usually solving the same problem with different hardware, green ramp instead of Talismans for the four-color mana, for example. The data gives you a second opinion, and here it mostly backs the plan.

For all four decks, the EDHREC numbers match the advice I gave. When the crowd and my swaps agree, that is a good sign the plan works.

Privacy

A quiet website, by design

This work is about safety, and that begins with how your visit is treated. In plain language: this site is built to collect as little as possible — ideally nothing at all.

Last updated: 2026

What this site collects

As published, this website does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, cookies, fingerprinting, embedded chat widgets, or third-party scheduling software. It does not build a profile of you, and it does not sell or share information about you, because it does not gather any.

Fonts are served from this site itself rather than a third-party font network, so simply reading these pages does not hand your visit to another company.

If you choose to email

The contact links open your own email application with a message addressed to the practitioner. If you write, the practitioner receives whatever you choose to share, in order to reply. Please send only what you are comfortable sharing by email, and avoid including sensitive health details you would prefer to keep private.

This site does not use a web form to gather emotional disclosures. That is deliberate: reflective prompts here are meant for your own private thought, not to be submitted to anyone.

Hosting

The site is served as static files by its hosting provider. Like most web hosts, the provider may keep basic server logs (such as IP addresses and requested pages) for security and reliability. That processing is governed by the host's own policies.

If features are added later

Should a newsletter, booking tool, analytics, or contact form be introduced in future, this page will be updated to describe what each one collects and why, and any such tool will be clearly disclosed before it is used.

Your choices

Because nothing is collected by the site itself, there is nothing to opt out of here. You remain in full control of what — if anything — you send by email.

Questions

Questions about privacy are welcome. Please reach out at hello@emotional-alchemy.example placeholder email.

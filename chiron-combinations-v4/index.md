# How Chiron Reads Card Combinations — v4

A two-card lookup into the Chiron Intuition corpus. Pick two cards; every passage shown is verbatim from her videos, source-linked, ranked by relational specificity (direct, clarifier, sequence, nearby).

## What v4 adds over [/chiron-combinations](/chiron-combinations)

- **Structural lens (Joan Bunning, *Learning the Tarot*, Lesson 15 — Card Pairs).** Every pairing is classified by card identity, separate from the transcript evidence:
  - **Permanent pair** — fixed opposites Bunning names (Magician / High Priestess; Eight of Swords / Four of Wands).
  - **Court-card / Ace pair** — two courts or two Aces, a contrast of suit and rank.
  - **Reinforcing pair** — same suit, energies compound rather than oppose.
  - **Occasional pair** — any two cards read together for one instance, no fixed opposition.
- **Featured pairing on load** — opens on Two of Cups with its most-documented partner, so the first screen shows a real reading instead of an empty prompt.
- **Methodology panel** — source date, coverage (2822 of 3003 pairs, 94%), ranking rules, classification, and limits moved out of the hero into a collapsible panel.
- **Honest empty state** — uncovered pairs read "No recorded passage yet."

The earlier build stays untouched at [/chiron-combinations](/chiron-combinations).

## Source

Corpus collected from 446 Chiron Intuition transcripts through July 7, 2026. Structural lens follows Joan Bunning, *Learning the Tarot* (Weiser, 1998), Lesson 15.

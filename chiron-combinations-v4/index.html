Methodology, sources, and what this does not cover

Source

Corpus collected from 446 Chiron Intuition transcripts through July 7, 2026. Every passage is verbatim and links to the exact video. A grounding pass confirmed each read traces to its cited source.

Coverage

This build indexes 2822 of the 3003 possible unordered pairs (94%) across the 78 cards, drawn from 26,169 combination passages, about 9.3 per pair. Pairs she was never recorded reading show “No recorded passage yet” rather than an invented reading. The only material excluded is a bare co-mention with no interpretation.

How passages are ranked

Software detects the card names, groups the passages by pair, and orders them by relational specificity: direct reads first, then clarifier, then sequence, then nearby. Every sentence stays verbatim; the ordering is automated, not human-edited. Contradictory reads are kept, because Chiron reads the same pair differently across subjects and questions.

The structural lens (Bunning)

Separate from the transcript evidence, each pairing is classified by Joan Bunning's Learning the Tarot, Lesson 15:
  • Permanent pair — fixed opposites Bunning names, such as the Magician and the High Priestess.
  • Court-card / Ace pair — two courts or two Aces, read as a contrast of suit and rank.
  • Reinforcing pair — two cards pushing the same energy rather than opposing it (here, same suit).
  • Occasional pair — any two cards read together for one instance, with no fixed opposition.
This lens is derived from card identity, not from the transcripts, so it is labeled as an interpretive frame, not as something Chiron said.

6 · Risk register

What can hurt you

  • Account health: open/undelivered orders → Etsy cases → search suppression.
  • Price cliff: the $1→$13 jump is untested; velocity could collapse.
  • Single point of failure: one human records every reading. July's 31 orders + manual fulfillment is exactly how the backlog was born — and volume is still climbing.
  • Trust erosion: storefront advertising 4 dead product lines.

What's working (protect it)

  • Proven demand: 63 orders in 9 weeks, accelerating, no ad spend cited.
  • A clear winning niche: psychic / love / no-contact readings.
  • Perfect rating: 5.0 across the 3 reviews you do have.
  • Built inventory: 121 listings ready to deploy the moment you choose a second leg.
  • Clean API pipeline: full read/write toolchain already in place for pricing, SEO, and fulfillment automation.

Marvel Super Heroes · Special Products

The Scene Boxes

Two collector boxes, twelve brand-new borderless legends. Line the six cards in a box side by side and their art fuses into one panoramic battle.

What a Scene Box is: each box (sold separately) holds 6 foil borderless legendary creatures found only here, three Play Boosters, six art cards, an oversized art print, and a display easel. The six cards' full-art frames connect edge to edge into a single cinematic image. They're built for the shelf first, the battlefield second, a couple still sneak into real Commander decks.

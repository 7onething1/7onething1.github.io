# The Scene Boxes — Marvel Super Heroes Commander

Two collector boxes, twelve brand-new borderless legendary creatures. Each box holds **6 foil borderless legends** exclusive to the product, three Play Boosters, six art cards, an oversized art print, and a display easel. The six cards' full-art frames connect edge to edge into one panoramic battle scene.

## Heroes United (Box 1)
Captain America rallies Earth's Mightiest into one sweeping charge.

1. **Captain America, Unbowed** (W) — Soldier-typal anchor; clean pickup for go-wide Soldier decks.
2. **Iron Man, Futurist Paragon** (U) — blue artifact-creature leaning on tech and equipment.
3. **Thor, Guardian of Midgard** (R) — big red Asgardian beater.
4. **Hulk, Always Angry** (G) — ★ oversized smasher that wipes every artifact on enter; the set's best shot at real Commander play.
5. **Black Widow, Intel Expert** (U/B) — Dimir espionage and value.
6. **Hawkeye, Trick Shot** (R) — red precision-damage archer.

## Villains Unleashed (Box 2)
Doctor Doom, Thanos, M.O.D.O.K., Loki and company dominate a full comic-book menace.

1. **Doctor Doom** (U/B/R) — the marquee villain presiding over the tableau.
2. **Thanos, Death's Consort** (B) — mono-black death and sacrifice.
3. **M.O.D.O.K., Evil Intellect** (U/B) — sizeable payoff for the draw-two archetype.
4. **Absorbing Man and Titania** (R/G) — ★ Gruul couple that doubles all combat damage from your creatures.
5. **Abomination, Irradiated Brute** (R/G) — Gruul bruiser built to hit hard.
6. **Loki, God of Lies** (R) — enables temporary steal-and-sacrifice mischief.

## Commander relevance
Most Scene cards are shelf-first collectibles. The two with genuine Commander upside are **Hulk, Always Angry** and **Absorbing Man and Titania**; **M.O.D.O.K.** and **Captain America, Unbowed** slot into their respective archetypes.
